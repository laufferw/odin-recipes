Baked Ziti

Baked Ziti is a hearty and comforting dish made with pasta, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. It is a classic Italian-American dish that is perfect for a family meal or a potluck.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ziti or penne pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 14.5-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds more, until fragrant.
  5. Add ground beef or Italian sausage and cook until browned, breaking up the meat with a spoon.
  6. Drain any excess grease from the skillet.
  7. Stir in crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, or until sauce has thickened.
  9. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta, ricotta cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Spread 1/2 of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  11. Top with pasta mixture and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese.
  12. Pour remaining meat sauce over top and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  13. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  14. Uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until bubbly and heated through.
  15. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
  16. Enjoy!
