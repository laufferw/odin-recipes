My Delicious Lasagna

This mouth-watering dish features layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and bubbling mozzarella cheese. Perfect for a hearty family meal or special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices (basil, oregano, salt, pepper)
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk, nutmeg)
  • Mozzarella cheese
